Kidney Health Information

The Kidney Dialysis Centre in Faridabad offers vital services for individuals with kidney-related issues. Kidneys act as essential filtration organs. They purify blood, maintain water balance, regulate electrolytes, and support overall body health.

Functions of the Kidneys:

  • Excretion of nitrogenous waste products.
  • Maintaining Hemoglobin (Hb) level.
  • Maintaining Calcium level for healthy bones.

What do healthy kidneys perform?

  • Balance body water levels.
  • Remove toxins and waste products.
  • Maintain chemical balance in the body.

How kidneys work:

  1. Renal arteries supply blood to the kidneys.
  2. Nephrons filter blood.
  3. Waste products form urine.
  4. Clean blood returns to the body.
  5. Urine travels to the bladder through ureters.
  6. Bladder releases urine from the body.
